## Phase 1: Seed Users (Week 1-4) — Zero Budget

**Goal: 10-20 real paying users through personal network**

1. **Personal outreach** — Talk to 50 SMB owners you know (friends, family, local market). Offer first 3 months free or ₹99/month founding member price
2. **Local market walk** — Visit 2-3 market areas (electronics market, cloth market, wholesale hubs). Demo on your phone. Focus on Udhar Khata pain point — that's the hook
3. **WhatsApp groups** — Join 10-15 business/trader WhatsApp groups in your city. Share 30-sec screen recordings showing Hindi voice input + credit tracking. Don't spam — answer questions, share value
4. **Success metric**: 10 active tenants using the product daily

## Phase 3: Paid Acquisition (Month 2-4) — ₹50K-1L/month

**Goal: 100+ paying users**

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) — ₹30-50K/month

- **Target**: Small business owners, 25-50 age, Tier 2/3 cities
- **Interest targeting**: Tally, Khatabook, Vyapar, GST, wholesale
- **Ad format**: Short video demo in Hindi showing Udhar Khata
- **CTA**: "Free trial — WhatsApp pe contact karo"
- **Landing**: buzeye.com with WhatsApp chat button

### Google Ads — ₹20-30K/month

- **Keywords**: "CRM for small business India", "udhar khata app", "GST invoice software", "credit management app Hindi"
- **Landing page**: Feature-specific (Udhar Khata page, GST page)

### WhatsApp Business API

- Use Buzeye's own WhatsApp integration to nurture leads
- Auto-reply when someone messages
- Demo scheduling via WhatsApp

---

## Phase 4: Partnerships & Scale (Month 3-6)

1. **CA/Accountant partnerships** — They recommend tools to SMB clients. Offer ₹500 referral per paid signup
2. **Trade association talks** — Free "Digital business management" workshop at local trader associations. Demo Buzeye at the end
3. **Reseller model** — Local IT shops/computer centers who set up software for shops. 20% revenue share
4. **Integration plays** — Tally export, Razorpay integration for payments
